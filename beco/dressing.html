Apart from studies, SABECO also helps develop skills of their students. Here we are going to highlight on the Dressing code, Sport activities, Musical and Instrumental Activities on the campus. It is mostly enjoyed by the Internal students (Boaders)

# Dressing in SABECO

The official dressing colors for Saint Bede's are: A White short handed top over a Blue down with completely Black Sobo Sandals or Shoe

Dressing For Boys:

For boys dressing it’s a white shirt tucked in, over a Blue long trouser with a black or white sucks, and completely Black sobo Sandals or completely black shoes and either a Blue needed plover with only white straps for first cycle or a white plover with only blue straps on it.

Dressing For Girls

Girls dressing is a white short sleave over a Pladded Blue skitrwith white sucks and ablack sobo snadals or black ballaries with a blue hair scarf(welly tierd on the hair) and either a Blue needed plover with only white straps for first cycle or a white plover with only blue straps on it.

Compound Wear

For boys it is a white shirt stucked into a black trouser with a black soucks and black sandals

For girls it is a white pladded gown with a blue hair scarf

Sporting Activities

Sporting here in SABECO is compulsory for both Male’s and Female’s. SABECO is a large school occupying a very large amount of land, with this, they have a field for each sporting activity. It consist of ;

  1. 3 Major football fields allowing a minimum of 2 football games going on at a time
  2. A long tennis court
  3. A basketball field
  4. A volley-Ball court
  5. Table tennis
  6. 2 Handball fields
